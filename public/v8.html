AIRWAVES
A FINGER-TAP PIANO IN THE AIR

It's laid out like a keyboard: low notes on the left half of the screen, high on the right. Put a hand on each side — left plays Do Re Mi Fa, right plays Sol La Si Do, ascending left→right. To sound a note, drop that finger down to your thumb's line; open your whole hand to stop — the thumb is just the line. Hand tracking runs 100% on-device (MediaPipe + WebGPU); synth is pure Web Audio.

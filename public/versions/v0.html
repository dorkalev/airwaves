PLAY MUSIC WITH YOUR BARE HANDS

Raise a hand — height picks the note, pinch to swell it, slide sideways to open it up. Two hands = two voices in harmony. It's quantized to a scale, so you literally can't play a wrong note. Hand tracking runs 100% on-device (MediaPipe + WebGPU); the synth is pure Web Audio.

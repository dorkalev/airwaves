AIRPIANO
A PIANO YOU PLAY IN THE AIR

A keyboard sits on the lower half of the screen. Move your fingers over it and touch a key to play it — white keys and black keys, polyphonic, hold for as long as you like. Any fingertip counts, so you can play chords with both hands. Hand tracking runs 100% on-device (MediaPipe + WebGPU); synth is pure Web Audio.

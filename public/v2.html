AIRWAVES
A FINGER-TAP PIANO IN THE AIR

Hold your thumb to a finger to sound its note (release to stop). Right hand: index→Do, middle→Re, ring→Mi, pinky→Fa. Left hand: Sol · La · Si · Do. Together that's a full octave — tap out a melody, or hold two hands for harmony. Hand tracking runs 100% on-device (MediaPipe + WebGPU); synth is pure Web Audio.

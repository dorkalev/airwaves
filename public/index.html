AIRLOOPER
LOOP REAL SOUND, WITH YOUR HANDS

A layer on top of reality: record your voice or guitar from the mic and stack loops — no buttons. Hover a hand over a pad and pinch (thumb + finger) to record one loop; it starts looping in time. Pinch it again to clear. Build a beatbox bed, hum a bassline, layer a riff. A click keeps you in time.
🎧 Use headphones — with speakers the mic will re-record the loops (feedback). Hand tracking is 100% on-device.
